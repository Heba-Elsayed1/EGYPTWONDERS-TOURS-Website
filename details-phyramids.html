Day Trip to Great Pyramids - Program:

Morning:

  1. Pick-up from your hotel in Cairo or Giza by our friendly and knowledgeable tour guide.
  2. Enjoy a comfortable drive to Giza, with our guide providing fascinating insights into the history and significance of the pyramids.
  3. Arrive at the Giza Plateau and witness the awe-inspiring sight of the Great Pyramids, one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World.

Mid-morning:

  1. Explore the iconic pyramids up close, with ample time for photo opportunities against these ancient marvels.
  2. Our complimentary professional photographer will accompany you to capture beautiful photos of your unforgettable journey.

Lunch

Savor a delicious lunch at a local restaurant, offering traditional Egyptian cuisine and flavors.

Afternoon:

  1. Continue the journey to the bustling bazaars of Cairo or Giza, where you can shop for souvenirs and immerse yourself in the vibrant local culture.
  2. Experience the richness of Egypt's heritage through a visit to a papyrus institute or a perfume shop, learning about ancient arts and crafts.

Evening:

As the day winds down, enjoy a relaxing drive back to your hotel, cherishing the memories you've made throughout the day.

Note:

The program can be tailored to your preferences and interests, ensuring you have a personalized and memorable experience. Our free photographer will ensure that you have stunning pictures to cherish and share with your loved ones.

Remember to book in advance to secure your spot and take advantage of the fantastic 50% discount for the second person! Let us take care of the details, while you enjoy the magic of Egypt's ancient wonders together.
