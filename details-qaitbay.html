Day Trip to Qaitbay Castle - Program:

Morning:

  1. Your adventure begins with a pick-up from your hotel in Alexandria by our friendly and knowledgeable tour guide.
  2. Embark on a comfortable drive to Qaitbay Castle, while our guide shares fascinating historical insights along the way.

Mid-morning:

  1. Arrive at the majestic Qaitbay Castle, an impressive fortress overlooking the Mediterranean Sea.
  2. Explore the captivating architecture and immerse yourself in the rich history of this medieval stronghold.
  3. Our complimentary professional photographer will be there to capture beautiful photos of your unforgettable journey.

Lunch:

Delight in a delicious lunch at a local restaurant, savoring the flavors of authentic Egyptian cuisine.

Afternoon:

  1. Continue your journey to discover more of Alexandria's charm, from the bustling streets to its cultural hotspots.
  2. Visit the famous Alexandria Library or explore the vibrant local markets, where you can find unique souvenirs.

Evening:

As the day draws to a close, our guide will escort you back to your hotel, ensuring you have cherished memories and stunning photos to treasure.

Note:

The program can be customized to suit your preferences, ensuring you have a personalized and unforgettable experience. Our free photographer will capture the magic of your journey, creating lasting memories to share with your loved ones.

Don't miss this opportunity to explore the wonders of Qaitbay Castle - book now and seize the chance to immerse yourself in history, culture, and breathtaking views with a photographer to capture the cherished moments along the way!
