Day Trip to Mohamed Ali Palace - Program:

Morning:

  1. Begin your day with a pick-up from your hotel in Cairo or Giza by our friendly and knowledgeable tour guide.
  2. Embark on a comfortable drive to Mohamed Ali Palace, with our guide providing captivating historical insights along the way.

Mid-morning:

  1. Arrive at the splendid Mohamed Ali Palace, an architectural masterpiece nestled atop the Citadel of Cairo.
  2. Explore the opulent interiors and admire the breathtaking views of the city below.
  3. Our complimentary professional photographer will be there to capture beautiful photos of your unforgettable journey.

Lunch:

Indulge in a delicious lunch at a nearby restaurant, savoring a taste of authentic Egyptian cuisine.

Afternoon:

  1. Continue your adventure in Cairo by visiting other renowned landmarks or experiencing the local culture in bustling bazaars.
  2. Uncover the art of papyrus making or enjoy a visit to a perfume shop, where you can discover the secrets of Egyptian scents.

Evening:

As the day comes to a close, our guide will accompany you back to your hotel, ensuring you have cherished memories and stunning photos to remember the day by.

Note:

The program can be tailored to your preferences and interests, ensuring you have a personalized and unforgettable experience. Our free photographer will capture the essence of your journey, providing lasting memories to share with your loved ones.
