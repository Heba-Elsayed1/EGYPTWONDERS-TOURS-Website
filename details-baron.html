Day Trip to Baron Palace - Program:

Morning:

  1. Our friendly and knowledgeable tour guide will pick you up from your hotel in Cairo or Giza.
  2. Set off on a comfortable drive to Baron Palace, as your guide shares intriguing stories about the historical significance of this opulent palace.

Mid-morning:

  1. Arrive at the Baron Palace, a breathtaking architectural gem, and step into a world of pure luxury and elegance.
  2. Take your time to explore the beautiful gardens, extravagant halls, and ornate rooms that showcase the palace's grandeur.
  3. Our complimentary professional photographer will be there to capture beautiful photos of your unforgettable journey.

Lunch:

Enjoy a delightful lunch at a nearby restaurant, savoring delectable dishes inspired by Egyptian cuisine.

Afternoon:

  1. Continue your journey to immerse yourself in the rich local culture at bustling markets and bazaars in Cairo or Giza.
  2. Experience the art of ancient papyrus making or visit a perfume shop, where you can discover the secrets of creating scents.

Evening:

As the day draws to a close, our guide will escort you back to your hotel, ensuring you have cherished memories to take home.

Note:

The program can be tailored to your preferences and interests, making it a personalized and unforgettable experience. Our free photographer will ensure that you have stunning pictures to cherish and share with your loved ones.
